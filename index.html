WELCOME TO TETSUYA

Awardwinning cuisine since 1974.Tetsuya is a Japanese restaurant with traditional roots, quality ingredients, and proper execution, with a belief in simplicity and details throughout the entire space.

A FAVORITE DESTINATION

With over 46 years in the area, we offer some of the finest food at our Sushi Bar, and Tetsuya Grill. Enjoy a lavish Dining Room with accents that highlight our culture and traditions.

A UNIQUE EXPERIENCE

Upon entering the space, the attention to details and design are clearly visible. The clean contemporary lines with natural woods create a sense of warmth and casual comfort. It is complimented by the chefs’ vision of simplicity in the food with a few touches of modern creativity.

TASTE SOMETHING NEW EVERY TIME

We pride ourselves in offering a wide selection of Japanese cuisine. From fresh sushi and sashimi, to ramen and delicious sake. We serve authentic Japanese cuisine with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

THE ATMOSPHERE

Our dining room is warm, comfortable and elegant, with beautiful shades of wood.You can always hear the sound of our guests talking and laughing and enjoying the Tetsuya experience. Our guests feel like family to us, and we’re confident that once you dine here, you’ll want to visit us again and again.

OUR VISION

At Tetsuya, we make every decision with the highest regard for this world and the people in it. From transparency in how we source our food to reducing our carbon footprint to enriching the communities we live in; our mission is to change the way people eat, inspiring a shift within the restaurant industry and beyond.
